Exceed or deceed 12

If you exceed the column sum of 12 the column that doesn't fit will appear on a row below and whitespace will be filled up in the spaces.

8

If you deceed the column sum of 12 the column will float to the left and whitespace will fill out the rest of the row.

Gutters

The gutters has the following format: [property]-[position]-[size]

<div class="m-b-2"> ... </div>

Properties:
m - Margin
p - Padding
Positions:
a - All
t - Top
r - Right
b - Bottom
l - Left
x - X-axis (right, left)
y - Y-axis (top, bottom)
Sizes:
0 - 0rem
1 - 0.5rem
2 - 1rem
3 - 1.5rem
4 - 3rem

Typography

All typography is scaled based on a modular scaling variable. By default the modular scaling variable is "Perfect fourth" (0.333)
